Laser cut Parts

Creating side components:

First, i sketched out the sides based on rough estimates of the size i want.Then I used the extrude tool to bring it from 2d to 3d.

Then I copied and pasted the side to create an identical opposite side.

Creating front,back and base components:

Then I sketched the front part, and similarly i used the extrude tool to make it 3d.

Then , cut the front out from the side to create the edges. Similiarly, i sketched the back part and extruded it.

Then i cut it out again to create the edges.

For the base, I created the sketch on the base and extruded it upward to form that part.

Creating middle and top components:

The middle component was tricky and required me to create a new plane to sketch on. After creating the plane, i drew the middle plane slanted so the ball is able to roll on it.

Creating a plane for the top part , then sketching the top part which is has sort of a odd shape compared to the other parts.

Creating start divider and slider:

Drawing on the middle plane, then extruding to create the starter divider to separate the launcher and the rest of the game.

Doing similiar to create a slope / slider for the ball to roll after the game ends.

Creating curve and other divider components:

I reference the two surfaces of middle and back in order to customise the curves at the edge of the box.

Then i sketched on the middle surface for the different parts to fit as well as for the smaller dividers to fit in.

Using extrude to create holes on the surface.

Finalising the design with the holes so that everything is able to fit.Then finally using sketches to export these as a dxf.
