Week 6 - 3D printing

This week we learnt about 3D printing and our exercises revolved around that.To start off the exercise we had to do was to create a chess piece.

First, I drew the border which was rectangle to estimate the size of the piece. Then I found an image of a queen chess piece and got the .svg of the file.

Next, I moved on to fitting the queen chess piece into the border, then i sectioned the different parts of the piece by using lines across the border.

Then, I proceeded to sort of trace the svg and change it here and there to create the chess piece.

This is how the clean sketch of the queen chess piece looks like. Then I use the revolve tool to form the chess piece in 3D.

Then , it looked a little weird so I used the fillet tool to smoothen out the piece. Finally, I finished off by shelling the chess piece and now it is ready for 3D printing.

The chess piece as done in fusion 360.

Then the next part was to print 3D print it.
