Phone Stand

For this assignment, we had to create a phone stand and here below documents my process in creating one. This image shows the requirement of the assignment.

The First step i did was to draw out the rough shape of the phone stand using sketch. Then I used the extrude feature to make it turn from a 2D drawing into a 3D model.

Next, I wanted to add a design to it, I went with a start shape and drew it on the back of the phone holder using sketch and then with the help of extrude , I made a one in the shape of a star.

Then , I wanted to add a more useful function to it, thus I came up with the idea of putting sort of a hanger for wired earpieces. Similarly, I used sketch and extrude to create this.

After this, I created a portion at the front of the phone holder for where the charger can be plugged into the phone. Again , this was done with the sketch and extrude function.

Then, grooves were added for more details as well as another star on the base of the phone holder.

To finish off this model, I used the fillet tool to round off any sharp edges.
