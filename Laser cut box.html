Laser Cut Box

For this weeks class, we had to design and build a laser cut box. The specific requirements are here below:

Firstly, I sketched and extruded the sketch to form the base of the box.

The next step was to create the sides of the box, similary I sketched it out and then used the extrude tool. This is how I formed the longer sides of the box.

After this, I did the shorter sides of the box using the same steps.

As seen here, i used the cut tool in order to cut the slots into the sides, this way the components could fit each other after laser cutting.

Then, I had to create the middle section of the box , this was to create a portion for where my drawer was going to go.

After creating the middle section, I cut a rectangle out the front of the box in order to form an opening for the drawer.Then using the same dimensions to ce=ut the rectangle, I drew out the front of the drawer.

Then I used the extruding tool to form the front. Similarly, I used the sketch and extrude again for the sides of the drawer.

To finish off the drawer, i added in the back and base of the drawer, making sure that I also cut out the extra parts that overlapped.Then i sketched a handle using lines and arcs.

I completed the handle by extruding it and even cut slot holes for the handle to fit into the drawer front. Next, I moved on to get more creative with the exterior of the box.I added these designs as an .svg

Then I had to create the opening of the box. I decided to go with flaps on the top.I sketched out how i wanted it to look like and then brought it to life with the extrude function.

I initially forgot to add the hinges, so I added it for the opening of the box afterwards.
